60-Day Money Back Guarantee

We stand behind every product we sell. If you are not satisfied for any reason within 60 days of your purchase, we will refund you in full — no questions asked.

1. Our Commitment

At Abundance Boost, we believe in the quality of our products. That's why we offer a full 60-day money back guarantee on all purchases. If our products don't meet your expectations, you deserve your money back — simple as that.

2. Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund:

  • Your refund request must be submitted within 60 days of the original purchase date.
  • You must contact us using the email address associated with your order.
  • Refund requests submitted after 60 days from the purchase date will not be eligible.

3. How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is straightforward:

  • Send an email to alivenerf.customers@gmail.com
  • Include your full name and the email address used at checkout.
  • Include your order number if available (found in your purchase confirmation email).
  • We will process your refund within 3 to 5 business days.

You do not need to provide a reason for your refund request, though feedback is always welcome.

4. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method used at checkout. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or card provider but typically appear within 3–7 business days after approval.

5. Digital Product Nature

Because our products are digital and delivered instantly, we are not able to "return" the files themselves. However, our 60-day guarantee still applies fully — we trust our customers and honor every valid request.

6. Questions

If you have any questions about your order or this policy, please don't hesitate to reach out: alivenerf.customers@gmail.com
